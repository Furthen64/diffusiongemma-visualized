1. Parallelism and Generation Order in Masked Diffusion Language Models

This is essentially a broader version of the DiffusionGemma investigation.

It studies eight diffusion language models, up to 100B parameters, and measures:

Which positions become final first.
How many positions become final together.
Kendall’s τ for generation order.
How generation behavior changes between knowledge, reasoning and programming tasks.

It even finds interesting cases such as Sudoku, where models tend to fill the easiest blanks first, rather than moving left-to-right. That is exactly the sort of “the whole canvas is available, so what order does the model choose?” question you are interested in.
